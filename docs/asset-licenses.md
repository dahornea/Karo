## Tooling

Sharp is used as a development-only renderer to convert versioned SVG source artwork to WebP. Sharp is software tooling, not an artwork source. Its package license remains governed by its upstream distribution.

When adding externally sourced assets in the future, record the exact URL, author, license version, attribution text, modifications, and date retrieved before merging. Assets with uncertain provenance must not be added.
